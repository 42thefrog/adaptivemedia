# Voice

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Tone | concise, composed, assured; assumes taste rather than teaching it |
| Do | short well-formed lines, precise word choice, let restraint carry the message |
| Avoid | generic phrasing, over-explanation, exclamation, anything that reads as a template |

# Notes

Camille discards whatever looks generic or over-explained. The design should
feel like an editorial spread, not a dashboard: calm, spacious, one refined
accent doing the work. Quality of composition signals more to her than volume
of content — five perfect words beat a paragraph.
