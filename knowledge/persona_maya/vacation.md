# Trip summary

Five days in a cheap gîte an hour outside Paris, rented with four friends from
42 the week after finals — half a break, half a self-imposed game jam. The deal
was simple: ship one tiny game by Sunday, and every time the build passed CI,
someone opened a beer. Hoodies never came off, the kitchen table became a war
room of laptops and whiteboard scribbles, and the group chat ran nonstop —
part study room, part comedy club. The bug that ate all of Friday night became
the running joke of the whole trip.
